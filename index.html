Loving Scientist

Magic Math Master was founded by Dr Tuyet Vu – a scientist, engineer, experienced maths teacher, and devoted mum of two.

She has spent decades in STEM education and industry, so she knows both how maths is used in the real world and how young minds learn best.

At Magic Math Master, we build on Dr Tuyet’s expertise to design lessons that are practical, engaging, and child-focused.
